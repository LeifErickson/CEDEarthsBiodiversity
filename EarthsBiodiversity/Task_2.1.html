The IUCN, in coordination with DENR are searching for the next generation ambassadors for environmental welfare in the Philippines. The ambassadors’ responsibilities are to explore the Biological diversity of species and to study the various issues, problems and solutions for bringing a sustainable ecosystem to the next generation. This serve as an opportunity for those aspiring individuals who want to be an ambassador if the Philippines but especially for those who are willing to learn about diversity of species on earth.

On the right is an open letter come from the office of secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR). This is an invitation to those aspiring young advocates who are willing to be part of nationwide advocacy for Environmental welfares.
