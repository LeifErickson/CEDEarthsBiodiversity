About
This WebQuest is created for the purpose of allowing Grade 8 learners to experience an inquiry-based learning about Biodiversity topics such as Species diversity, Hierarchical taxonomic system of classification and Protection and conservation of endangered and economically important species. Furthermore, it is an avenue for them to develop 21st century skills and gain mastery of the intended content of each lesson after completing various tasks in this activity.
